New Discoveries

Fresh finds with a real use case

Mirage

Stream a Mac window, full desktop or secondary display to another Apple device.

Best for: Power users who want flexible second-screen and remote-display workflows.

Homepage

DockFreeze

Lock the macOS Dock to one display so it stops jumping around multi-monitor setups.

Best for: Anyone running multiple displays who wants one tiny fix for Dock behavior.

Homepage

Docomments

Attach Markdown notes to files and folders using Finder metadata that remains Spotlight-searchable.

Best for: People who need lightweight context notes without maintaining a separate file database.

Homepage

Trending

Apps power users are likely to talk about

SonicFlow

A free open-source per-app volume mixer for macOS with separate sliders for active audio sources.

Best for: Users who miss Windows-style app audio controls on the Mac.

Homepage

Dockspace

Add previews, widgets and controls directly to the Dock.

Best for: Large-display users who want the Dock to become active workspace instead of a static launcher.

Homepage

Mailway

Route mailto links to the right native app or webmail account using recipient and domain rules.

Best for: People splitting work and personal email across multiple clients.

Homepage

AI & Automation

Utilities for faster, more automatic Mac work

Auto Ducking

Automatically lowers background audio whenever your microphone becomes active.

Best for: Calls, dictation, recording and voice-first AI workflows.

Homepage

Gemini Spark for Mac

Google's Mac assistant is beginning to act on shared local files and connected apps.

Best for: Users tracking the move from chatbot shortcuts toward desktop agents.

Homepage

Mobile Builds

Track Xcode Cloud build status, history and failures from a focused Mac and iOS app.

Best for: Apple developers who want build awareness outside the browser.
